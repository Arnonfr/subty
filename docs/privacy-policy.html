Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Subty ("we", "our", or "us") is a subtitle search and translation app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Subty does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers. We do not have user accounts, and we do not track your activity.

2. API Keys You Provide

To use translation features, you may optionally enter a Google Translate API key. This key is stored locally on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences and is never transmitted to us or any third party other than the Google Translate API.

3. Third-Party Services

Subty uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • OpenSubtitles API – used to search and download subtitles. See OpenSubtitles Privacy Policy.
  • Google Translate API – used for subtitle translation (requires your API key). See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Remote Config – used to manage app availability. No personal data is collected. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Local Storage

Subty stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Search and translation history (stored in a local database)
  • App settings (dark mode, preferred languages, etc.)
  • Downloaded subtitle files (saved to your Downloads folder)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

5. Permissions

Subty requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET – required to search and download subtitles and to perform translations.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – used to continue translation while the app is in the background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – used to show translation progress notifications (Android 13+).
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – used to save translated subtitle files to your Downloads folder.

6. Children's Privacy

Subty is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or by updating the date above.
